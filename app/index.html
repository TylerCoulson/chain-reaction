How to Play

Find all the words in the chain

  • You are given the first word in the chain
  • The next word will be part of a two-word phrase or compound word
  • Words will be at least 3 letters
  • After a correct guess the first letter of the next word will be shown
  • Incorrect guesses will add a letter to the word and be marked red

Example

  • STOP
  • LIGHT
  • RAIL
  • YARD
  • STICK
  • FIGURE
  • EIGHT
  • BALL
  • GOWN
